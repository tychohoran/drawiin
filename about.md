## About the Space

Drawiin دراوين is a new artist-run space, collective design studio, and community library in Carthage, Tunisia focussed on environmental communication. Our studio is nestled between a small pine forest and a field of wheat in [Carthage Mohamed Ali](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BHiF3FjfSYJnk5wh6){:target="_blank"}.

# ![The Forest Behind the Studio](/uploads/forest.jpg)

We collaborate closely with other artists, organizations, and our neighbors to produce prints, publications, landscape designs, films, workshops, pop-up events, and more. Remembering that cultural production is always political, we always work in tandem with movements for environmental justice and collective liberation.
